Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 28, 2025

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how The Governing Church (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information through our mobile application (“the App”). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our App:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, profile photo (if uploaded), prayer requests, testimonies, or form submissions.
  • Non-Personal Information: Device details (model, operating system, version), app usage data (pages visited, duration, features used), and anonymous analytics for app improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our church app services
  • Send updates, devotionals, and event notifications
  • Respond to prayer requests, messages, or feedback
  • Manage member and volunteer registration
  • Enhance communication between members and the church leadership

3. How We Protect Your Information

We apply appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no digital system is entirely secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your information may only be shared:

  • With trusted partners or service providers who assist in operating the App under confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or government authority
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of The Governing Church, its members, or the public

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent or opt out of communications

To make any such request, please email us at thecuttingedgeoffice@gmail.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App is designed for users of all ages, including families. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links or integrations with third-party services (such as YouTube, Google Maps, or payment gateways). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their respective privacy policies.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date. Your continued use of the App after any modification means you accept the updated terms.
